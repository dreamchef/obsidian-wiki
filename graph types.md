#combinatorics 

- Graph: No loops or multiple edges, diagraph where whenever there is an arc $(x,y)$, there is also an arc $(y,x)$
- Digraph: No loops or multiple arcs
- Multigraph
- Multidigraph
- Subgraph
- Induced subgraph
