#analysis 

If $\sum_{k=1}^\infty a_k = A$ and $\sum_{k=1}^\infty b_k = B$, then
1. $\sum_{k=1}^\infty (a_k + b_k) = A + B$ and
2. $\sum_{k=1}^\infty ca_k = cA$ for all $c \in \mathbb{R}$
