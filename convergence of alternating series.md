#analysis 

If an [[alternating series]] satifies
1. $b_n \geq n_[n+1]$ for all $n \geq N$ for some fixed $N$
2. $lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} b_n = 0$

then the alternating series $\sum_{n=1}^\infty (-1)^{n+1} a_n$ [[converge]]s.
