(of a matrix $X$), the result of subtracting the mean from $X$ to give $Y$, and taking $Y^T Y$. Alternatively,

$\Sigma = 
\begin{bmatrix}
	Var(X) & Cov(X,Y) \\
	Cov(X,Y) & Var(Y)
\end{bmatrix}$
