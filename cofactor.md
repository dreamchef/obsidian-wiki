#linear-algebra #concept

the (i,j) cofactor of an n-by-n matrix A is the appropriately sgned [[determinant]] of the matrix created byt removing the ith row nad jth column from A
$$C_A(i,j) = (-1)^{i+j}det(A_{:~i,:~j})$$
